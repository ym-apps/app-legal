Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the FaangIQ app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Yubraj (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".


Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with privacy in mind and operates entirely offline. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.


Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Your display name (if you choose to provide one)
  • Quiz progress and statistics (questions completed, correct answers, streaks)
  • Category-specific performance data
  • Quiz history (last 10 quiz sessions)
  • Application settings (haptic feedback, sound preferences, notifications)


Offline-First Design: The Application operates completely offline. All quiz questions, your progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage technology. No data is sent to remote servers, and no account or email address is required to use the Application.


The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information with the Service Provider or any third parties. Your data remains on your device at all times.


The Application does not gather any information about your device's location.


Backup and Export

The Application provides optional backup and export features that allow you to:

  • Export your progress data to a JSON file stored on your device
  • Import previously exported data to restore your progress
  • These files remain under your control and are not transmitted to any servers
  • You can delete these backup files at any time using your device's file manager

Third Party Services

Production Builds: When you download the Application from the App Store or Google Play Store, no data is collected or transmitted by the Application or the Service Provider.


Data Access

The Application requires the following permissions on your device:

  • Storage: To save your progress, settings, and quiz history locally on your device
  • File System Access (Optional): Only when you choose to export or import backup files

The Application does NOT require or request:

  • Internet/Network access (works completely offline)
  • Camera access
  • Contacts access
  • Location services
  • Microphone access

Opt-Out Rights

You can delete all data collected by the Application at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Progress" option in the Profile tab (this is irreversible)
  • Uninstalling the Application from your device

You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. Uninstalling the Application will permanently delete all locally stored data.


Data Retention Policy

All data is stored exclusively on your device and remains there until you:

  • Manually delete it using the "Reset All Progress" feature
  • Uninstall the Application
  • Clear the Application's data through your device settings

The Service Provider does not have access to your data and cannot retrieve or restore deleted data.


Children

The Application does not collect any personally identifiable information and is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under 13. Since no data is transmitted or collected by the Service Provider, there are no privacy concerns for children using this Application.


The Application can be used by anyone, including children under the age of 13. No personal information is collected, and all data remains on the device. Parents and guardians can review the Application's functionality and are encouraged to supervise their children's use of any mobile applications.


Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. The Service Provider recommends:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device-level security features (passwords, biometric locks, etc.)
  • Being cautious when exporting backup files to ensure they are stored securely
