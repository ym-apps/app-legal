Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection to use FaangIQ?

No! FaangIQ works 100% offline. All questions and your progress are stored locally on your device. You can practice anywhere without worrying about internet connectivity.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved as you complete quizzes. Visit the Stats tab to see detailed analytics including:

  • Total questions completed
  • Correct answer percentage
  • Current streak
  • Category-wise performance
  • Recent quiz history

Can I choose specific categories to practice?

Absolutely! FaangIQ allows you to select from 13+ categories including Algorithms, Data Structures, System Design, Behavioral Questions, and more. You can also filter by difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).

How do I backup my progress?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Export Progress" to save your data as a JSON file. You can later import this file to restore your progress if you switch devices or reinstall the app.

What if I accidentally reset my progress?

Unfortunately, if you haven't exported your data before resetting, it cannot be recovered as all data is stored locally on your device. We strongly recommend using the Export feature regularly to backup your progress.

Can I customize the quiz settings?

Yes! In the Profile tab, you can customize:

  • Number of questions per quiz (5-50)
  • Haptic feedback on/off
  • Sound effects on/off
  • Notification preferences
  • Theme (Light/Dark mode)

How accurate are the questions compared to real FAANG interviews?

Our questions are carefully curated based on real interview patterns and commonly asked topics at top tech companies. While we strive for accuracy, actual interview questions vary by company and role. Use FaangIQ as a comprehensive study tool alongside other resources.

Will my data be synced across devices?

Currently, FaangIQ stores all data locally on your device. There's no automatic cloud sync. However, you can manually transfer your progress between devices using the Export/Import feature.

How often is the question bank updated?

We regularly review and update our question bank to ensure relevance and accuracy. Updates are released through app store updates. Make sure to keep your app updated to access the latest content.

Tips for Success

  • Practice consistently - aim for at least one quiz daily to maintain your streak
  • Focus on understanding explanations, not just memorizing answers
  • Review your quiz history to identify weak areas
  • Start with easier questions and gradually increase difficulty
  • Use the category filter to strengthen specific topics
  • Export your progress regularly as a backup
