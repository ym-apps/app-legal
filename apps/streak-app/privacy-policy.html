Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 1, 2026

Short version: ClearStreak collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. We never see it.

1. Who We Are

ClearStreak ("the App") is a habit and streak tracker built by Yubraj (YM Apps). Contact: yubrajkhatri977@gmail.com

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your habit names, streaks, or logs
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any information about you whatsoever

3. Where Your Data Lives

All data you create in ClearStreak — habits, streaks, logs, settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). It never leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no cloud sync.

If you uninstall the app, all your data is permanently deleted from your device.

4. Notifications

ClearStreak can send you daily reminder notifications if you choose to enable them. These are local notifications scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to or processed by any server.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

ClearStreak does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data platforms. The only third-party dependency is the Expo SDK, which powers the app framework. Expo's privacy policy is available at expo.dev/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

ClearStreak does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
