Frequently Asked Questions

My streak reset unexpectedly. What happened?

In Strict mode, missing a single day resets the streak to zero — this is by design. If you prefer to count total completions instead of consecutive days, switch the habit to Count mode in the habit settings.

Can I move my data to a new phone?

Yes. Go to Settings → Backup (JSON) to export a full backup of your habits and logs. On your new phone, open ClearStreak and use Settings → Import to restore everything.

I am not receiving reminder notifications.

Make sure you have granted notification permissions to ClearStreak in your device Settings. On iOS, go to Settings → ClearStreak → Notifications and enable Allow Notifications. Reminders are set per habit — edit the habit to confirm a reminder time is saved.

I deleted the app and lost all my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately no. ClearStreak stores all data locally on your device only. We have no servers and no access to your data. Uninstalling the app permanently removes it. We recommend using the JSON backup feature regularly.

Does ClearStreak work offline?

Yes, completely. ClearStreak requires no internet connection to function. All data is stored on your device.

How do I delete a habit?

Swipe left on any habit card from the home screen to reveal the delete option.
