Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my favorite verses?

Tap the heart icon next to any verse to add it to your favorites. You can access all your saved verses from the Favorites tab.

What is AI Krishna?

AI Krishna is an AI-powered chatbot that helps answer your spiritual questions and provides guidance based on Bhagavad Gita teachings.

Do I need an internet connection?

The core Bhagavad Gita content is available offline. However, features like AI Krishna and cloud sync require an internet connection.
